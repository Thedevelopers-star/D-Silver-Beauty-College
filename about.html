Our Journey

Founded on the principle that beauty is both a science and an art, D-Silver Beauty College began as a vision to bridge the gap between passion and professional excellence.

Over the years, we have transformed from a boutique training center into a prestigious academy, recognized for producing stylists and therapists who lead the industry in Kenya.

Our curriculum is designed not just to teach techniques, but to cultivate the creative soul of every student who walks through our doors.

Our Founder

Mr Sudi

Founder & Chief Visionary

With years of expertise in the beauty industry, Mr Sudi envisioned D-Silver Beauty College as a sanctuary for aspiring beauty professionals. His passion for excellence and commitment to transforming lives through education has been the driving force behind our institution's success.

Mr Sudi's leadership philosophy centers on empowerment, innovation, and the belief that every student deserves world-class training in a nurturing environment. Under his guidance, D-Silver has evolved into a beacon of beauty education excellence in Limuru and beyond.

His unwavering dedication to quality instruction and industry standards ensures that every graduate leaves D-Silver equipped with both technical mastery and the confidence to excel in their careers.
